About Me

I am a current graduate student pursuing my masters in data science and analytics. Throughout my undergraduate degree as an economics major I learned about the importance of analyzing data in my econometric courses. This sparked my interest in pursuing further studies in data analysis. As part of my masters I have obtained a variety of skills with tools such as SQL,Python, R and building a dashboard with Power BI and Tableau.

Apart from my studies, sports and athletics have always been a significant part of my life. I played basketball for the University of Calgary and have used my love for sports to contribute to my work in data analytics. Additionally, I continue to coach basketball camps in my local community outside of my analytical work.
